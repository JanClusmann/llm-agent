8. Develop Risk Reduction Strategies: For each hazardous substance, develop strategies to reduce the risk. This could include implementing safety measures, changing work practices, or using less hazardous alternatives.

9. Document the Findings: Record all the information gathered and the decisions made during the risk assessment. This will provide a record of the process and can be used for future reference.

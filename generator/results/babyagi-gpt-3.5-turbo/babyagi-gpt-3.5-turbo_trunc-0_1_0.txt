9. Partner with schools and workplaces: Collaborate with schools and workplaces to incorporate cancer prevention education into their curriculum or wellness programs. Offer to conduct presentations or provide educational materials to raise awareness among students, employees, and their families.

10. Measure the impact: Regularly evaluate the effectiveness of your information campaign. Use surveys, feedback forms, and social media analytics to gather data on audience engagement, knowledge gain, and behavior change.
